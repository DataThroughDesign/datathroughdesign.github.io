DATA THROUGH DESIGN DATA THROUGH DESIGN DATA THROUGH DESIGN


Data Through Design was an art exhibit showcasing works based on open data under the theme 8 Questions for the City. The mission of Data through Design and Open Data Week, a city-wide endeavor by the NYC Mayor’s Office of Data Analytics, is to encourage a wider knowledge of New York City’s open datasets and how these resources can enable better public understanding of life in the city. Collectively, these works reveal insights from a cumulative whole and what stories can emerge from data. The exhibition is co-organized by the Pratt SAVI, CARTO, Enigma, and will be hosted at the Made in NY Media Center.
